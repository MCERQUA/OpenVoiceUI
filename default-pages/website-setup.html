Local Service SEO Page Structure
Every service company website needs this structure for local SEO:
/services/ - top-level services list
/services/roof-repair/ - individual service page
/service-areas/ - all service areas list
/service-areas/phoenix/ - area page (near-duplicate of services)
/service-areas/phoenix/roof-repair/ - area + service combo
/blog/ - blog index
/faq/ - FAQ page
Each service area page should be a near-duplicate of the services page, re-worded specifically for that area. Each service under each area gets its own page with area-specific content. This creates area x service unique pages for maximum local search coverage. Services also exist at the top level (not under any area) as the canonical version.
